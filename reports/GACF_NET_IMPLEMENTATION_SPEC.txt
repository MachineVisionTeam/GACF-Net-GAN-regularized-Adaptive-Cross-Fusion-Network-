3. TOTAL COMPUTE USED
================================================================================

For the AGAFNet replication + head-to-head experiment specifically (the
focus of this work). GACF-Net Stage 1 + Stage 2 training times are
documented in GACF_NET_HYPERPARAMETERS_SPEC Section 4 and Section 2.2.

  Phase                           | Wall-clock time on this machine
  --------------------------------|----------------------------------
  AGAFNet replication training:
    Lizard  (pre-existing)        | ~7.5 hr  (5 folds, sequential)
    CoNSeP  (this work)           | ~3 hr    (5 folds, 4-GPU parallel)
    PanNuke (this work)           | ~6 hr    (3 splits, 3-GPU parallel)
  AGAFNet inference + post-proc  |
    Lizard / CoNSeP / PanNuke     | ~2-3 min per fold (sequential)
  Step 0 post-processing          | ~1-3 min per dataset (all images
                                  | watershed-segmented at once)
  Step 1 match + label table      | ~1-3 min per dataset
  Step 2 handcrafted (32 workers) | ~2-5 min per dataset
  Step 3 UNI extraction (1 GPU)   | ~1-4 min per dataset
  Step 4 per-fold scaling         | seconds
  Step 5 GACF-Net Stage-2 train   | ~10-60 sec per fold
  Step 6 XGBoost head-to-head     | ~5-10 min per dataset

Total head-to-head experiment across all 3 datasets (excluding the
pre-existing Lizard AGAFNet replication): ~12 hr.
